To start a hold, first select the turn direction and leg length. Then, press the Start Auto Hold button and the aircraft will preform IFR hold indefinitely based on your current heading and position using the Infinite Flight Autopilot. To stop the hold, press the Disengage Auto Hold Button.

You are still the pilot in command, and you can disengage the hold at any time by pressing the Disengage button or exiting the page. This tool is meant to assist pilots in flying holds, but it is not a replacement for manual flying and decision making. flightSmart is not responsible for any incidents or violations that may occur from using this tool, so please use it wisely and always maintain situational awareness.
